## Introduction

<p>
	The Resolve BVN service helps you build a KYC profile for a Nigeria-based
	customer using their BVN.
</p>
<p>
	This service has two tiers: Standard and Premium. The Premium tier provides
	all the information in the Standard tier, as well as more granular information
	for a more detailed KYC profile.
</p>
<Callout type="info" title="Pricing">
	BVN Resolve Standard costs <strong> N10 per successful call</strong> while BVN
	Resolve Premium costs <strong>N15 per successful call</strong>.
</Callout>
